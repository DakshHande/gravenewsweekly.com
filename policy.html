Monetization Policy

At GraveNews Chess Weekly, we believe in providing free and accessible chess content for everyone. We do not charge any fees or require subscriptions to view our articles.

Our revenue is generated solely through advertisements displayed on our website. These ads help us maintain the site and continue delivering high-quality chess news and analysis.

We ensure that the ads displayed are relevant and non-intrusive, allowing for an uninterrupted browsing experience.
