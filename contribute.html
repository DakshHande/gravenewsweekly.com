How You Can Contribute

At GraveNews Weekly, we believe in the power of community. Whether you're a chess enthusiast, a web developer, or someone who loves to network, there are many ways you can contribute to our mission. Here's how you can get involved:

1. Talk About Us on the Internet or in Public

  • Share our website and articles on social media platforms like Twitter, Facebook, and Instagram.
  • Mention GraveNews Weekly in your blogs, podcasts, or YouTube videos.
  • Recommend us to your friends, family, and fellow chess players.

2. Help Us in Web Development

  • If you're a web developer, designer, or tech enthusiast, we'd love your help in improving our website.
  • Contribute to our open-source projects on GitHub.
  • Suggest new features or report bugs by contacting us at support@gravenews.com.

3. Join Our Chess Team and Tournaments

  • Become a part of the GraveNews Academy Team on Lichess and participate in daily tournaments.
  • Improve your chess skills by competing with players from around the world.
  • Click here to join: Join the GraveNews Academy Team.

4. Network with GraveNews (The CEO)

  • Connect with GraveNews, the CEO, to discuss collaborations, partnerships, or mentorship opportunities.
  • Follow GraveNews on social media for updates and insights:
    • Twitter: @gravenews
    • LinkedIn: GraveNews
  • Reach out via email at gravenews@gravenews.com.

Why Contribute?

By contributing to GraveNews Weekly, you become a part of a growing community of chess lovers and innovators. Your support helps us create better content, improve our platform, and organize more exciting events. Together, we can make GraveNews Weekly the ultimate destination for chess enthusiasts worldwide.
